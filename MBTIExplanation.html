INTJ - The Architect

INTJs are strategic thinkers who excel at planning and achieving long-term goals.

INTP - The Thinker

INTPs are analytical and curious, driven by a love of knowledge and new ideas.

ENTJ - The Commander

ENTJs are natural-born leaders, known for their determination and strategic thinking.

ENTP - The Debater

ENTPs are innovative, curious, and thrive on exploring new ideas and engaging in debates.

INFJ - The Advocate

INFJs are insightful and altruistic, often working to make the world a better place.

INFP - The Mediator

INFPs are empathetic and imaginative, striving to create harmony and find deeper meaning in life.

ENFJ - The Protagonist

ENFJs are charismatic leaders who inspire others through their vision and empathy.

ENFP - The Campaigner

ENFPs are enthusiastic and creative, excelling in building meaningful connections with others.

ISTJ - The Inspector

ISTJs are reliable and practical, with a strong sense of duty and commitment to traditions.

ISFJ - The Defender

ISFJs are nurturing and protective, dedicated to supporting others and preserving harmony.

ESTJ - The Executive

ESTJs are organized and efficient leaders, excelling at managing people and processes.

ESFJ - The Consul

ESFJs are warm and caring, with a deep focus on fostering connections and community values.

ISTP - The Virtuoso

ISTPs are curious and hands-on, excelling at problem-solving and crafting innovative solutions.

ISFP - The Adventurer

ISFPs are sensitive and creative, often seeking beauty and new experiences in their surroundings.

ESTP - The Entrepreneur

ESTPs are energetic and bold, thriving in fast-paced environments and enjoying risks and challenges.

ESFP - The Entertainer

ESFPs are fun-loving and spontaneous, bringing excitement and energy to every situation.
